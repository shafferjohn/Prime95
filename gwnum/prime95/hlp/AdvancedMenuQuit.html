How to Use Prime95: Advanced Menu - Quit

 

ADVANCED MENU - Quit

Quit

The Quit GIMPS menu choice is used when you no longer want this computer to work on the GIMPS project. You may rejoin at a later date. If you are a PrimeNet user your unfinished work will be returned to the server. If you are a manual user, you need to send me email containing your results.txt file and a note saying you are quitting.
