NEW FEATURES IN VERSION 8.x of PRIME95.EXE

New Features in Version 8 of Prime95

  1. Factoring speed has been doubled for Pentiums. 486 machines will notice a 15% improvement when factoring.
     
  2. The program will now automatically determine your CPU type and speed. This information is used in choosing the optimal factoring algorithm.
